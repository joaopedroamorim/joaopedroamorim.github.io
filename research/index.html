Sargassum Blooms on Amazon Coast

During May 2014 and Apr 2015, several tons of Sargassum sp bleached on Amazon Coast as reported on local news . During this project, I investigated how ocean currents, near surface winds and regional precipitation could driven and transport these algae from a region above the equator to northern Brazil coast.
